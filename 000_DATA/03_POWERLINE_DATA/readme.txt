Data are downloaded from here: https://overpass-turbo.eu/

Click on "Wizard" and then input:

type:way & (power=line | power=cable | power=minor_line) in Alberta

OR

type:way & (power=line | power=cable | power=minor_line) & (voltage = 500000 | voltage = 240000) in Alberta

Click "Build and Run Query"

All the powerline data should appear in the map window

Then click "Export" and download as KML
